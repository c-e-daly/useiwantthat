# What is Markup Performance?

## Definition

Markup Performance is the discipline of pricing retail products with a defended floor — COGS plus Profit Markup — and a flex zone above it built from The Five Allowances: Shrink, Financing, Shipping, Discounts, and Market Adjustment.

## How Markup Performance works

Markup Performance separates pricing into two layers:

1. **The defended floor** — COGS plus Profit Markup
2. **The flex zone** — the funded allowances above the floor

The defended floor protects the economic viability of the business. The flex zone absorbs operational volatility and market pressure without collapsing margin.

Traditional margin-based pricing tends to treat all markup as one undifferentiated buffer. Markup Performance decomposes markup into named operational functions so operators know exactly where pricing pressure is being absorbed.

## Why Markup Performance matters in 2026

Most ecommerce operators are running businesses built for a lower-volatility environment than the one that exists today.

Shipping costs fluctuate faster, financing costs are materially higher, promotional pressure is constant, and AI-assisted shopping introduces real-time competitive pricing pressure. The old model — “set a margin target and hope the quarter works” — breaks under those conditions.

Markup Performance gives operators a pricing structure designed for volatility instead of stability.

## How Markup Performance differs from margin management

Margin management measures financial outcomes after the sale. Markup Performance structures pricing before the sale.

Margin management asks: “What happened to gross margin?” Markup Performance asks: “Was the pricing system funded correctly to begin with?”

One is reactive accounting. The other is operational pricing architecture.

## How to apply Markup Performance to your store

1. **Separate your defended floor from your flex zone** instead of treating markup as one buffer.
2. **Fund all five allowances explicitly** based on operational reality.
3. **Track allowance consumption separately** so margin pressure becomes diagnosable.

## FAQ

**Q: What is Markup Performance in retail pricing?**  
A: Markup Performance is a pricing framework that separates the defended floor of pricing from the operational allowances that absorb volatility and market pressure.

**Q: Why is Markup Performance important now?**  
A: Ecommerce volatility increased substantially after 2021 through higher financing costs, shipping volatility, acquisition pressure, and AI-assisted pricing competition.

**Q: Is Markup Performance the same as margin optimization?**  
A: No. Margin optimization measures financial outcomes after transactions occur. Markup Performance structures the pricing system before the transaction happens.
