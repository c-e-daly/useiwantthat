Negotiated Commerce Fit Assessment

Negotiated commerce fit assessment

This tool scores whether your store is operationally and philosophically ready for customer generated offers. It is not asking whether you like discounts. It is asking whether you understand price elasticity, customer portfolios, margin protection, and the value hidden inside customers who do not buy at the current price.

How do you view discounts?
Negotiated commerce treats discounts as pricing allowances and opportunity cost, not automatic margin destruction.
How do you think about customers?
The model works best when you believe different customers create value in different ways.
How do you manage acquisition?
If CAC is rising, increasing customer yield from existing traffic becomes a strategic lever.
How do you price products?
Strong fit requires understanding COGS, markup, and allowances like shipping, shrink, finance, and markdown room.
How do you handle inventory and clearance?
Negotiation is especially useful when fixed sale prices leave money on the table.
How do you evaluate performance?
The manifesto favors customer portfolio management over simple cohort thinking.
How do you feel about agentic commerce?
Future fit improves if you want AI agents and shoppers to know your store can receive offers.
What is your operating mindset?
This system is for operators willing to test, measure, counter, and learn.
