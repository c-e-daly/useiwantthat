Programs  
Align your inbound customer generated offer strategy to your outbound strategy. Set accept and deline rates, discount prefixes, expiry minutes and discount stacking.

Storefront Offers  
Strategic Placement of the Wanna Make A  Deal button on the site. Use templates of your products page or set it sitewide \- you are in control at all times.

Ai Agentic Offers  
Enable accepting AI Agentic Offers through Vector.  Steal business from competitors and win more customers using Agentic Generated Offers through Vector.

Price Builder  
Negotiated Commerce requires the deployment of allowances to arm your shop with the tools and levers of conversion for a broader selection of customers.  Allowances shield your profits while flexing your offerings to win more business.

Counter Offers  
It’s a negotiation platform, not a discount platform. Counter offers enable you to structure what YOU want and relay it back to the customer in real time or over a few days.  Negotiation is a two way street \- counter offers are your toolkit.

Automated Counters  
Attach counter offers to programs and start automating the process to meet your business goals on margin performance, markup performance, inventory movement or customer value creation.  

Real Time Offer Analytics  
Every single offer, every single counter offer, analyzed in retail time with a simple gauge to determine the drivers of profits and the efficacy of allowances to drive conversion.

Campaigns  
Layered programs to target different customer portfolios, different business objective and even different collections and categories.  You can run multiple concurrent programs on acquisition, reactivation and clearance with different decision criteria.

Agentic Counter Offers  
Your programs, campaigns, offers, counter offers, customer portfolios feed a machine learning algorithm and we expose planning goals in the app for the machine to take the wheel, layout campaigns and programs, summarize demand forecasts and automated the counter offers in real time with our offer intelligence agency.  Your data is your own, private and specifically for what works for your brand.
