**Feature sections**

*Offers — let customers tell you what they'll pay* Stop guessing at the price that converts. When a customer makes an offer, Prophet evaluates it against your margin floors and financial goals in real time. Accept the right offers automatically. Decline the rest without lifting a finger. No spreadsheets. No daily decisions. Just sales that protect your business.

*Counter Offers — close the gap, keep the margin* A customer offers $42. Your floor is $51. Without Prophet, they leave. With Prophet, they get a counter at $54 — automatically or from you — and they buy. Every negotiation you were losing to a bounce becomes a conversion. At a price you chose.

*Price Builder — know your floor before any offer arrives* Free shipping isn't free. Discounts aren't free. Prophet's Price Builder makes every cost explicit — COGS, shipping, handling, platform fees — so you always know the minimum price that keeps your business healthy. Set your floor once. Never accidentally accept a losing deal again.

*Programs — align your offers to your calendar* BFCM is coming. Q1 is slow. You're running a Meta campaign next week. Programs let you configure exactly how aggressive Prophet gets — and when. Widen acceptance thresholds before a push. Tighten them when demand is strong. Your offer strategy adapts to your business, not the other way around.

*Remarketing — re-engage without paying for the click twice* You already paid to bring them to your store. When they don't buy, Prophet gives them a reason to come back that isn't a coupon code. A personalized offer invitation — "name your price" — brings back lapsed visitors on their terms. You get the conversion. Meta doesn't get the retargeting fee.

*Exit Intent — we don't capture emails. We capture orders.* Every exit popup in existence asks for an email address. Prophet asks for something more valuable: what would you actually pay? A visitor who names a price is a buyer. We capture purchase intent at the moment it exists — before they're gone — and convert it into a sale.
