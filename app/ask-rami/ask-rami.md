## How It Works

### 1. Tell Rami What You Want

Search, paste a product link, or describe what you are looking for.

Rami helps organize the request and turns your shopping intent into action.

### 2. Set Your Price

You decide what you are willing to pay.

That could be based on budget, urgency, prior purchases, comparable products, or simply what the item is worth to you.

### 3. Rami Handles the Offer

When a participating store supports offers, Rami can help submit your offer and receive the seller’s response.

### 4. You Decide What Happens Next

You stay in control.

You can:

- Accept the deal
- Review a counteroffer
- Save the item for later
- Move on

No pressure. No endless retargeting. No fake urgency.

## Three Modes of Buying

Ask Rami is designed to grow with you.

### Manual Mode

You control the shopping process.

You tell Rami what you want, where you want to shop, and what you are willing to pay.

Rami helps execute the request.

### Co-Pilot Mode

Rami starts helping more actively.

He can suggest products, track prices, organize shopping lists, and help manage upcoming purchases.

### Autopilot Mode

Eventually, Rami will be able to anticipate recurring needs, seasonal events, replenishment cycles, and household buying patterns.

You remain in control, but Rami does more of the work.
